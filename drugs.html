CIBLOMIX: biomedical AI; "Make sense of drugs data with biomedical knowledge"

SYNOPSIS


Huge quantity of drugs already exist on the medical market, but at the same time science is progressing. The non-use of certain drugs in a context different from their initial marketing is a reality. Artificial intelligence with biomedical knowledge connection refocus drugs in new therapeutic protocols.
